Buffalo Chicken Slider

Description

The best slider to slide onto your plate is one with buffalo chicken inside of it! And thats a fact. Like a buffalo chicken wrap, this dish does not require many ingredients or prep time. It makes for a perfect mid-day snack to hold you off until dinner. Continue down below to see how you can make one of these bad boys from the comfort of your own home! Can you tell how much I love buffalo chicken?!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • King's Hawaiian Rolls
  • Monterey Jack Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Toss sliced and cooked chicken breasts in buffalo sauce. Toss until chicken is coated.
  3. Cut rolls in half and add chicken and cheese
  4. Cover and bake for 10 minutes.
